# SINGAPORE JOB DATA ANALYSIS REPORT
## Salary Insights for Business Planning

**Analysis Date:** February 4, 2026  
**Dataset:** 1,044,583 cleaned job postings across 53,151 companies  
**Target Users:** HR Departments, HR Consultancy, Job Seekers, Government Agencies

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Key Findings:

- **Market Size:** 1,044,583 active job postings from 53,151 unique companies
- **Overall Median Salary:** SGD 3,800/month
- **Salary Range:** SGD 1 - SGD 464,000 (cleaned outliers)
- **Career Growth Potential:** Senior Management roles pay 4x more than entry-level (SGD 11,041 vs SGD 2,734)
- **Top Employment Type:** Contract positions offer highest average pay (SGD 5,019)

- **Internship programs** are effective talent pipeline with low cost
- **Flexible work** options need salary premiums to compete

## 3. SALARY BY POSITION LEVEL (Career Progression & Salary Growth Path)

| Position Level | Count | Mean Salary | Median Salary | Avg Experience |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Senior Management | 22,806 | SGD 11,041 | SGD 9,500 | 7.9 years |
| Middle Management | 27,373 | SGD 7,793 | SGD 6,500 | 5.6 years |
| Professional | 112,206 | SGD 7,112 | SGD 6,000 | 4.0 years |
| Manager | 110,121 | SGD 6,806 | SGD 6,000 | 5.1 years |
| Senior Executive | 100,458 | SGD 5,696 | SGD 5,000 | 3.9 years |
| Executive | 253,697 | SGD 4,134 | SGD 3,750 | 2.4 years |
| Junior Executive | 167,656 | SGD 3,407 | SGD 3,150 | 1.6 years |
| Non-executive | 131,605 | SGD 2,984 | SGD 2,700 | 1.7 years |
| Fresh/entry level | 118,661 | SGD 2,734 | SGD 2,550 | 0.5 years |

### Career Growth Insights:
1. **Clear salary ladder exists:**
   - Entry to Executive: **1.5x** (SGD 2,734 → SGD 4,134)
   - Executive to Senior Mgmt: **2.7x** (SGD 4,134 → SGD 11,041)

2. **Most competitive market segments:**
   - Executives (253,697 postings) - highest supply
   - Junior Executives (167,656 postings)
   - Non-executives (131,605 postings)

3. **Salary premium by level:**
   - Each management level → **20-30% salary increase**
   - Professional roles bypass Executive → **71% higher** salary

### For Job Seekers:
- Transitioning to **Professional track offers faster salary growth** than Management
- Management roles require **2-3 more years** experience for comparable compensation

## 4. SALARY BY EXPERIENCE LEVEL (Skills Valuation)

| Experience | Job Count | Mean Salary | Median Salary | Salary Growth |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0 years (Fresh) | 114,450 | SGD 2,969 | SGD 2,700 | Baseline |
| 1 year | 264,084 | SGD 3,240 | SGD 3,000 | +9% |
| 2 years | 219,909 | SGD 3,809 | SGD 3,500 | +28% |
| 3 years | 169,786 | SGD 4,830 | SGD 4,250 | +63% |
| 4 years | 34,982 | SGD 6,015 | SGD 5,500 | **+103%** |
| 5 years | 145,152 | SGD 6,354 | SGD 5,500 | +114% |
| 6 years | 20,244 | SGD 7,918 | SGD 8,000 | +167% |
| 7 years | 11,569 | SGD 9,394 | SGD 8,500 | +216% |
| 10 years | 26,604 | SGD 10,869 | SGD 10,000 | +266% |
| 12+ years | 2,621 | SGD 13,327 | SGD 12,000 | +349% |

### ROI of Experience:
1. **First 3 years = critical growth period:**
   - Year 0→1: +9% salary increase
   - Year 1→3: +49% cumulative increase
   - Year 0→3: **+63% total growth**

2. **Experience sweet spot: 4-5 years**
   - Double entry-level salary (103-114%)
   - Job supply still strong (180k+ positions)

3. **Senior experience (10+ years):**
   - 3.7x entry-level salary
   - Smaller job pool (26k positions)
   - Highly specialized roles

### For HR Cost Management:
- Invest in **junior talent development** (1-3 years)
- **4-5 year mark** = critical retention point (salary jumps to SGD 5,500-6,350)
- **Senior hires (7+ years)** require SGD 8,400+ to stay competitive

- Wider bands reduce **offer negotiation friction**

## RECOMMENDATIONS

### For HR Departments:
1. **Establish role-based salary bands** using 25th-75th percentile range
2. **Monitor Contract vs Permanent premium** - maintain 3-4% differential
3. **Accelerate junior talent retention** at 4-5 year mark with targeted raises
4. **Use experience scale** to structure internal progression paths

### For HR Consultancy Firms:
1. Package **10-20 role salary benchmarks** for client engagements
2. Develop **industry-specific salary guides** (IT, Finance, Retail)
3. Create **experience-based compensation matrices** for market positioning
4. Advise on **flexible work compensation adjustments**

### For Job Seekers:
1. **Target 4-5 year experience point** for 2x salary growth
2. **Consider Professional track** for faster career growth than Management
3. **Negotiate using 25th-75th percentile band** in job postings
4. **Plan career transitions** between levels to maximize salary progression

### For Government (WSG/MOM):
1. **Entry-level baseline:** SGD 1,500-2,550 (minimum wage benchmark)
2. **Skills value:** Experience commands **3-4x salary growth** over 10 years
3. **Employment diversity:** Permanent (44%) vs Contract (13%) vs Flexible (2.5%)
